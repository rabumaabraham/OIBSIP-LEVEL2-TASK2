Tribute to Edward Snowden

Explore the life and legacy of one of the most controversial figures of the 21st century.

Who is Edward Snowden?

Edward Snowden is a former NSA contractor who leaked classified information from the National Security Agency in 2013. His actions sparked a global debate over mass surveillance, privacy, and government transparency. Snowden’s revelations revealed the extent of the surveillance programs run by the U.S. government and its allies, impacting how people view government overreach in the digital age.

Key Accomplishments

★

Exposed global surveillance programs by the NSA.

🛠

Founded the Freedom of the Press Foundation to advocate for digital rights.

🏃

Received the Sam Adams Award for Integrity in Intelligence.

Legacy

Edward Snowden’s legacy remains a subject of intense debate. While some consider him a hero for his role in exposing government surveillance, others label him a traitor for his leaks of sensitive information. Regardless of perspective, Snowden’s revelations continue to shape discussions on privacy, civil liberties, and government transparency in the digital age.
